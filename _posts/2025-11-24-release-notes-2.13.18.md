[Scala 2.13.18](https://github.com/scala/scala/releases/tag/v2.13.18) is now available!

This release
fixes several regressions from 2.13.17.
It is compatible with JDKs 8 to 26.

For details, refer to the [release notes](https://github.com/scala/scala/releases/tag/v2.13.18) on GitHub.
