[Scala 2.12.21](https://github.com/scala/scala/releases/tag/v2.12.21) is now available!

This release is compatible with JDK 25 LTS and also introduces preliminary support for JDK 26.

For all the details, refer to the [release notes](https://github.com/scala/scala/releases/tag/v2.12.21) on GitHub.
